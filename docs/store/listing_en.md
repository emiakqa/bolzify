## Long Description (both stores, max 4000 chars)

```
Bolzify is the prediction app for the FIFA 2026 World Cup — and every tournament after.

THE IDEA
How well do you really know football? Take on your friends. Predict every World Cup match, name the winner and the top scorer, and watch the live league ranking battle it out.

HOW IT WORKS
• Predict match results — change your pick anytime up to kickoff
• Bonus prediction on the first goalscorer of each match
• Special tips before the tournament: champion, runner-up, semifinalists and top scorer
• Reminder one hour before kickoff — only if you haven't predicted yet

POINTS SYSTEM
For each match:
• 6 points for the exact result
• 4 points for the correct goal difference
• 2 points for the correct tendency
• +3 points for the correct first goalscorer

Special tips:
• 10 points for the champion
• 5 points for the runner-up
• 5 points per correct semifinalist (up to 4)
• 5 points for the top scorer

LEAGUES FOR FRIENDS
Create a league and share the six-digit code — everyone who joins competes in the live ranking. No size limits. Each league has its own table.

COMPLETELY FAIR
• Predictions lock at kickoff — no cheating
• Other players' tips are hidden until the match starts
• Points are awarded automatically as soon as the final score is in

PRIVACY FIRST
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Your data lives in the EU (Frankfurt)
• Delete your account anytime in settings — one tap
• Notifications are scheduled locally on your device only

WHO IT'S FOR
• Football fans who want to prove their forecasting skills
• Friend groups, flatshares, offices fighting for bragging rights
• Anyone who likes Kicktipp or Comunio but wants something modern

BUILT FOR THE 2026 WORLD CUP
48 nations battle for the title across the USA, Canada and Mexico. Bolzify is live from day one: fixtures, group stage, knockouts, all the way to the final at MetLife Stadium on July 19, 2026.

Get started — sign-up takes under a minute.
```
